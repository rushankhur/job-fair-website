Essential information

The IT Job Fair is held annually on the NSCC IT campus in Halifax.
Whether you have jobs today or will have jobs in the future, our students want to know more about your organization and potential careers. Meet and contact our talented students and alumni from several NSCC campuses on the same day.

We look forward to seeing you there in the next IT Job Fair!

Download the Industry Guide

Advantages for organizations:

Organizations can benefit greatly from taking on an NSCC student. Think about the value of having a work-ready student for extra support, or for special projects. You will have the opportunity to evaluate and identify potential future employees as part of your long-term recruitment strategy. This is a wonderful opportunity to foster the next generation of employees by providing them with valuable career-related work experiences.

Booking your Booth

2.1: Booking your booth will be done electronically via our website using our “Registration Form” unless otherwise stated. It is your responsibility to ensure the information you provide is accurate and up-to-date.

2.2: Should you need more than one booth, it's important that you contact us immediately to make arrangements. There are no guarantees you will receive an extra booth(s). This greatly depends on the availability.

Confirming your Booth

3.1: Confirmation of your booth for the job fair should take place no later than 7 days before the start date of the job fair.

3.2: Failure to confirm your spot by the specified date will result in your employer losing their spot at the event.

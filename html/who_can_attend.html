Who can attend?

The IT Job Fair was established to help NSCC Information Technology students find work terms during the spring, as completion of a work term at the end of each year of study is a requirement to graduate. NSCC Information technology students from all NSCC campuses are encouraged to attend this event.

NSCC IT Job Fair was a spectacular opportunity to learn about employment opportunities and talk with industry. I found that as a first year it was really helpful to see what employers are out there; it gave me a much better idea of what the job market is like!

Owen Pattison, a first-year IT Programming student and 2019 IT Job Fair Attendee

Why You Should Attend

  • To network with potential employers
  • To investigate and research career options
  • To obtain information from employers on
  • Career opportunities
  • Corporate cultures
  • Job requirements
  • Industry growth
  • Industry trends
  • Skills and qualifications

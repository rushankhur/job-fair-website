Join us for the IT Job Fair!

NSCC's largest college wide recruitment fair is here! With many organizations attending, you will find a wide variety of career opportunities and graduate programs.

The IT Job Fair is open to all NSCC students and alumni to discover amazing opportunities, and network with potential future employers, professional associations and programs.

IT Job Fair


The IT Job Fair offers direct contact between companies, institutions and students of NSCC helping to grow IT-industry.

Conference


The IT Job Fair consists of a variety of useful workshops and presentations by companies, organizations and NSCC Student Services.

Career-Related Programming

The IT Job Fair consists of programs such as speed interviews, and the Halifax Partnership Connector Program.

Our IT Job Fair

Worldwide enterprises exhibit on the NSCC IT Campus along with regional companies and freshly founded start-ups. At the 2019 IT Job Fair, 24 exhibitors presented information about job opportunities such as internships, co-op programs, entry level positions and research projects.

Employers

Our long list of previous employers includes large international companies such as IBM, Lockheed Martin Canada, CGI as well as regional firms, like REDspace, Maplewave, Sobeys, Nova Scotia Power and others.

Networking

The IT Job Fair has grown into a popular networking platform for exhibitors, students, and numerous alumni. Companies are invited to give presentations and meet some of the best upcoming talent the school has to offer.
